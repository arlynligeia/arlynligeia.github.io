BIOGRAPHY

Ligeia, also known as Arlyn Ligeia, is an independent multidisciplinary Peruvian artist focused on film, music, fashion, art, and science. Her creative universe blends psychology, physics, art, and eroticism, wrapped in a gothic-cosmic-futuristic aesthetic.

With a solid academic background, she holds a degree in Psychology and, as complementary training, has studied Fashion Design and Management.

In 2021, Ligeia directed her first audiovisual work, a music video for an independent band, marking the beginning of her path as a director. She directed the video via video call during the COVID-19 lockdown. In 2024, despite facing a severe economic crisis, she improvised with what she had at hand and managed to create her first short film. She wrote, directed, shot, starred in, and edited it herself.

After completing a music video and a short film, Ligeia is now in the production stage of her first feature film—entirely written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by her. At the same time, she is working on her debut album and her first fashion collection.

Ligeia draws inspiration from film noir, gothic cinema, and science fiction, as well as from vaporwave and cyberpunk aesthetics. She defines her art as authentic and visceral, a space where she freely explores female sexual desire. She admires the archetype of the femme fatale, a recurring theme in her work. She refuses to be pigeonholed and opposes any prejudice, convinced that true art arises from the fusion of diverse stimuli. She firmly believes that emotion and reason can coexist in perfect harmony.

ALONE FILM

UPCOMING

Written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by Arlyn Ligeia

SYNOPSIS

A horror content creator puts a retrocausal protocol to the test. What seemed like a simple experiment turns sinister when the rules of reality begin to break around her.

CURRENTLY IN PRODUCTION

This upcoming feature film marks Ligeia's debut in the world of cinema. Blending psychological and cosmic horror with a deeply dreamlike aesthetic.

Entirely self-produced, the film is a bold statement of artistic independence, shaped by her background in psychology and science.

The project was born out of adversity and a profound exploration of solitude during one of the most financially challenging periods of her life. And yet, rather than giving in, Ligeia found a way to turn pain into art.

This film is a love letter to artistic survival, to the loneliness that fuels creation, and to the relentless pursuit of one's deepest dreams, even in the darkest of times.

Filming is currently underway.
