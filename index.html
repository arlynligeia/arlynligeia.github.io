UNIVERSE

Welcome to the universe of Arlyn Ligeia

This is a space where the gothic, cosmic, and futuristic inhabit the same place, where the past and the future are the present, where emotion and reason form a single feeling, and where art and science intertwine with the same purpose, the expression and curiosity born from within.

For Ligeia, art is more than a medium; it is a language of the soul that is born from the inside out.

Every project, whether a feature film, a song, a garment, a jewel, a painting, a poem, or an article, is born from a personal need to explore, feel, and connect.

All of her works are fragments of the same inner universe that expands and intertwines, inviting the viewer to become a part of it.

Here, more than a collection of works, you will find the profound inner world of an authentic artist, true to herself, and an invitation to interact or become part of it.

It is a refuge for those who, like Ligeia, seek an authentic place.

ABOUT ARLYN LIGEIA

Ligeia, also known as Arlyn Ligeia, is an independent and multidisciplinary Peruvian artist focused on film, music, fashion, and art. Her creative universe fuses the gothic, cosmic, and futurism, wrapped in a unique aesthetic.

Ligeia grew up in Moquegua, Peru. While living there, her refuge was always films and music. She studied Psychology at the University, and as complementary training, Audiovisual Communication and Fashion Design and Management. She currently holds a **Bachelor of Science in Psychology** and a diploma in Fashion Design and Management.

In 2021, Ligeia directed her first audiovisual work, a music video for an independent band, which marked the beginning of her career as a director. In 2024, despite facing a severe economic crisis, she improvised with what she had on hand and managed to create her first short film. She wrote, directed, shot, starred in, and edited it herself. That same year, Ligeia applied and was admitted to the Master's in Performing Arts at the Pontifical Catholic University of Peru; however, she could not pursue her studies due to her financial crisis.

As a result of this, and after completing a music video and a short film, Ligeia is currently in the production phase of her first feature film, written, directed, filmed, starring, and edited entirely by her. Simultaneously, she is working on her debut album and her first fashion collection.

Ligeia draws inspiration from the gothic, the cosmos, and technology, as well as from retrofuturistic aesthetics, vaporwave, cyberpunk, and pop culture.

Ligeia's artistic trajectory is a conscious search for inner authenticity. She embraces her femininity along with her interests and passions, without rigidly separating them. Thus, in the face of neutrality and biases, Ligeia firmly chooses visual and sensory stimulation as an act of creative honesty and fidelity to herself.

Ligeia defines her art as authentic and visceral, a space where she freely explores female sexual desire. She admires the archetype of the femme fatale, a recurring theme in her work. She refuses to be pigeonholed and opposes any prejudice, convinced that true art arises from authenticity and the fusion of diverse stimuli.

FILM

Welcome to the Film section.

Here, more than a collection of works, you will find a map of Arlyn Ligeia's vision and interests. For the artist, cinema is more than a medium; it is a language of the soul. Each project, whether a feature film, short film, or music video, is born from a personal need to explore, feel, and connect. All of her works are pieces of the same interior universe that expands and intertwines, inviting the viewer to be a part of it.

Her artistic path is a conscious pursuit of intensity and emotion. Faced with the temptation of neutrality, Ligeia chooses visual and sensory stimulation as an act of creative honesty. Her filmography is not a statement against currents, but an expression of her being in favor of the life that pulses in excess, chaos, and the beauty that finds its own logic.

Cinema is the heart of her entire creative ecosystem. It is the space where the rational and the visceral meet, and where her images seek to transcend the screen to become complete experiences: in music, in fabrics, in textures, in atmospheres that can be breathed.

From this nucleus, the journey expands into fashion, music, art, and science, as a way of understanding the human. Each work functions as a node in this network, a starting point for a continuous sensory and intellectual exploration.

ALONE

UPCOMING

Written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by Arlyn Ligeia

SYNOPSIS

A horror content creator puts a retrocausal protocol to the test. What seemed like a simple experiment turns sinister when the rules of reality begin to break around her.

CURRENTLY IN PRODUCTION

This upcoming feature film marks Ligeia's debut in the world of cinema. Blending psychological and cosmic horror with a deeply dreamlike aesthetic.

Entirely self-produced, the film is a bold statement of artistic independence, shaped by her background in psychology and science.

The project was born out of adversity and a profound exploration of solitude during one of the most financially challenging periods of her life. And yet, rather than giving in, Ligeia found a way to turn pain into art.

This film is a love letter to artistic survival, to the loneliness that fuels creation, and to the relentless pursuit of one's deepest dreams, even in the darkest of times.

Filming is currently underway.

EL INFIERNO DE OZUL

EL INFIERNO DE OZUL is a silent short film written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by Ligeia. Created in 2024 as part of an academic examination during her Audiovisual Communication studies, the short pays homage to both silent and gothic cinema, combining classic references with a raw and deeply personal vision.

With very limited resources and facing economic hardship, Ligeia was forced to improvise with what she had at hand, transforming those limitations into creative strength. This short film stands as a testament to her belief in art as a means of survival.

DESPERTAR - BLUE

DESPERTAR this was Ligeia's first audiovisual creation, made for the Blue Band of Moquegua. The music video was produced in 2021, during the height of the pandemic. For its production, Ligeia chose three locations: two in the city where she lived and one in Lima.

Due to the health restrictions imposed by COVID-19, Ligeia independently managed the entire production. She contacted everyone who could contribute to the project, arranged the locations by phone, and coordinated every detail. She directed the entire storyline set in Lima remotely via video calls. For the scenes filmed in her own city, she was present in person, handling both directing and filming herself.

She also edited the entire music video on her own.

The video explores Gothic Romanticism as its central theme, delving into fantasy and deep emotions such as solitude, presented through a melancholic and evocative aesthetic.
