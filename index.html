Biography

Ligeia, also known as Arlyn Ligeia, is a multidisciplinary and independent Peruvian artist focused on film, music, fashion, art, and science. Her creative universe merges psychology, physics, art, and eroticism, wrapped in a gothic-cosmic-futuristic aesthetic, taking science fiction to a symbolic and visceral level. With a solid academic background, she holds a B.S. in Psychology and is currently studying Physics, challenging the prejudices that try to separate art from scientific thought. As complementary education, she also has training in Fashion Design and Management.

In 2021, she directed her first music video through a video call, during the pandemic and COVID-19 lockdown. In 2024, despite going through a severe economic crisis, she improvised with what she had at hand and managed to create her first short film: she wrote, directed, shot, starred in, and edited it herself. After completing a music video and a short film, she is now in the production stage of her first feature film, entirely written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by her. At the same time, she is working on her debut music album and her first fashion collection.
