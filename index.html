Welcome to the universe of Arlyn Ligeia

This is a space where the gothic, cosmic, and futuristic inhabit the same place, where the past and the future are the present, where emotion and reason form a single feeling, and where art and science intertwine with the same purpose, the expression and curiosity born from within.

For Ligeia, art is more than a medium; it is a language of the soul that is born from the inside out.

Here, more than a collection of works, you will find the profound inner world of an authentic artist, true to herself, and an invitation to interact or become part of it.

It is a refuge for those who, like Ligeia, seek an authentic place.

ABOUT ARLYN LIGEIA

Ligeia, also known as Arlyn Ligeia, is an independent and multidisciplinary Peruvian artist focused on film, music, fashion, and art. Her creative universe fuses the gothic, cosmic, and futurism, wrapped in a unique aesthetic.

Ligeia grew up in Moquegua, Peru. While living there, her refuge was always films and music. She studied Psychology at the University, and as complementary training, Audiovisual Communication and Fashion Design and Management. She currently holds a **Bachelor of Science in Psychology** and a diploma in Fashion Design and Management.

In 2021, Ligeia directed her first audiovisual work, a music video for an independent band, which marked the beginning of her career as a director. In 2024, despite facing a severe economic crisis, she improvised with what she had on hand and managed to create her first short film. She wrote, directed, shot, starred in, and edited it herself. That same year, Ligeia applied and was admitted to the Master's in Performing Arts at the Pontifical Catholic University of Peru; however, she could not pursue her studies due to her financial crisis.

As a result of this, and after completing a music video and a short film, Ligeia is currently in the production phase of her first feature film, written, directed, filmed, starring, and edited entirely by her. Simultaneously, she is working on her debut album and her first fashion collection.

Ligeia draws inspiration from the gothic, the cosmos, and technology, as well as from retrofuturistic aesthetics, vaporwave, cyberpunk, and pop culture.

Ligeia's artistic trajectory is a conscious search for inner authenticity. She embraces her femininity along with her interests and passions, without rigidly separating them. Thus, in the face of neutrality and biases, Ligeia firmly chooses visual and sensory stimulation as an act of creative honesty and fidelity to herself.

Ligeia defines her art as authentic and visceral, a space where she freely explores female sexual desire. She admires the archetype of the femme fatale, a recurring theme in her work. She refuses to be pigeonholed and opposes any prejudice, convinced that true art arises from authenticity and the fusion of diverse stimuli.

FEATURE FILM

FEATURE FILM

ALONE

UPCOMING

Written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by Arlyn Ligeia

SYNOPSIS

A horror content creator puts a retrocausal protocol to the test. What seemed like a simple experiment turns sinister when the rules of reality begin to break around her.

CURRENTLY IN PRODUCTION

ALONE is Arlyn Ligeia's first feature film. This is a very personal project for Ligeia, born from adversity and a profound exploration of solitude during one of the most financially challenging periods of her life.

Yet, rather than giving up, Ligeia found a way to turn pain into art. This film was written by Ligeia and will be directed, shot, starred in, and edited by her.

This feature film will mark Ligeia's debut in the world of cinema. A science fiction story blending psychological and cosmic horror with a deeply dreamlike aesthetic.

Entirely self-produced, the film is a bold statement of artistic independence, shaped by her background in psychology.

This film is a love letter to artistic survival, to the loneliness that fuels creation, and to the relentless pursuit of one's deepest dreams, even in the darkest of times.

Filming is currently underway.

RESEARCH ARTICLE

ORCID

Welcome to the Science section.

Arlyn Ligeia cultivates a deep passion for science, with a particular interest in the fields of psychology. Her vocation for research was consolidated during her university education, culminating in first place in the Fourth Research Projects Competition 2018 of the José Carlos Mariátegui University (Moquegua, Peru), thanks to her work titled "Personality and self-esteem according to preference for type of animated series, in university students of the City of Moquegua, 2018".

This line of research not only reflects her academic rigor but has been integrated as a fundamental pillar in her creative universe. Her intellectual production includes several specialized publications in psychology, available on academic platforms. For Arlyn, science is a constant source of inspiration and an essential component of her multidisciplinary artistic expression.
