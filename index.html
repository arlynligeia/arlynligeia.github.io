FEATURE FILM

FEATURE FILM

ALONE

UPCOMING

Written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by Arlyn Ligeia

SYNOPSIS

A horror content creator puts a retrocausal protocol to the test. What seemed like a simple experiment turns sinister when the rules of reality begin to break around her.

CURRENTLY IN PRODUCTION

ALONE is Arlyn Ligeia's first feature film. This is a very personal project for Ligeia, born from adversity and a profound exploration of solitude during one of the most financially challenging periods of her life.

Yet, rather than giving up, Ligeia found a way to turn pain into art. This film was written by Ligeia and will be directed, shot, starred in, and edited by her.

This feature film will mark Ligeia's debut in the world of cinema. A science fiction story blending psychological and cosmic horror with a deeply dreamlike aesthetic.

Entirely self-produced, the film is a bold statement of artistic independence, shaped by her background in psychology.

This film is a love letter to artistic survival, to the loneliness that fuels creation, and to the relentless pursuit of one's deepest dreams, even in the darkest of times.

Filming is currently underway.
