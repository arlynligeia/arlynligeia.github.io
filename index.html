BIOGRAPHY

Ligeia, also known as Arlyn Ligeia, is an independent multidisciplinary Peruvian artist focused on film, music, fashion, art, and science. Her creative universe blends psychology, physics, art, and eroticism, wrapped in a gothic-cosmic-futuristic aesthetic.

With a solid academic background, she holds a degree in Psychology and, as complementary training, has studied Fashion Design and Management.

In 2021, Ligeia directed her first audiovisual work, a music video for an independent band, marking the beginning of her path as a director. She directed the video via video call during the COVID-19 lockdown. In 2024, despite facing a severe economic crisis, she improvised with what she had at hand and managed to create her first short film. She wrote, directed, shot, starred in, and edited it herself.

After completing a music video and a short film, Ligeia is now in the production stage of her first feature film‚Äîentirely written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by her. At the same time, she is working on her debut album and her first fashion collection.

Ligeia draws inspiration from film noir, gothic cinema, and science fiction, as well as from vaporwave and cyberpunk aesthetics. She defines her art as authentic and visceral, a space where she freely explores female sexual desire. She admires the archetype of the femme fatale, a recurring theme in her work. She refuses to be pigeonholed and opposes any prejudice, convinced that true art arises from the fusion of diverse stimuli. She firmly believes that emotion and reason can coexist in perfect harmony.

SHORT FILM

EL INFIERNO DE OZUL

EL INFIERNO DE OZUL is a silent short film written, directed, shot, starred in, and edited by Ligeia. Created in 2024 as part of an academic examination during her Audiovisual Communication studies, the short pays homage to both silent and gothic cinema, combining classic references with a raw and deeply personal vision.

With very limited resources and facing economic hardship, Ligeia was forced to improvise with what she had at hand, transforming those limitations into creative strength. This short film stands as a testament to her belief in art as a means of survival.

MUSIC VIDEO DIRECTION

BLUE - DESPERTAR

DESPERTAR this was Ligeia‚Äôs first audiovisual creation, made for the Blue Band of Moquegua. The music video was produced in 2021, during the height of the pandemic. For its production, Ligeia chose three locations: two in the city where she lived and one in Lima.

Due to the health restrictions imposed by COVID-19, Ligeia independently managed the entire production. She contacted everyone who could contribute to the project, arranged the locations by phone, and coordinated every detail. She directed the entire storyline set in Lima remotely via video calls. For the scenes filmed in her own city, she was present in person, handling both directing and filming herself.

She also edited the entire music video on her own.

The video explores Gothic Romanticism as its central theme, delving into fantasy and deep emotions such as solitude, presented through a melancholic and evocative aesthetic.
